WELCOME TO OUR CHURCH COMMUNITY

At Methodist Church Rotterdam, we are more than just a congregation; we are a family. Our church offers a variety of groups and ministries designed to nurture your spiritual growth, build meaningful connections, and serve our community. Each of our major groups is dedicated to fostering fellowship, learning, and support within a welcoming and inclusive environment. Explore our major groups below and discover where you can find your place in our church family.

THE CHURCH CHOIR

Welcome to the heart and soul of Methodist Church's musical ministry - our Church Choir. Our choir is a vibrant and devoted assembly of voices that come together to create beautiful harmonies, uplifting the spirits of all who gather in worship.

WHAT WE DO

WEEKLY REHEARSALS

Join us for rehearsals to learn and perfect hymns, anthems, and other musical pieces that enhance our worship services on Fridays 1:00-2:30PM

SPECIAL PERFORMANCES

We participate in special church events, holiday celebrations, and outreach programs, sharing the joy of music with the entire community

MUSIC EDUCATION

We offer training and opportunities for vocal development, music theory, and the opportunity to embrace your musical talents
